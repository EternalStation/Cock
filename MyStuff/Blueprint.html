Neon Survivor - Master Concept Blueprint

1. Combat & Targeting

Player Mechanics

  • Movement: WASD / Arrow Keys. Physics-based movement (Speed boosted 15% to 4.85).
  • Targeting: Auto-aims at the nearest enemy. Grid View expanded to cover full map.
  • Stats:
    • Health: Auto-regens over time (based on reg stat).
    • Attack Speed: SCORE Based. Base 200 = 1s delay. Cap 9999 = 0.02s delay. (Higher is Faster).
    • Damage: Base 50.

  • Global Speed: All entities boosted +15% to compensate for clock normalization.
