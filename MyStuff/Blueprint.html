Advanced Game Mechanics

Enemy Scaling Logic

  • Spawn Rate: 1.2 + (0.1 × Minutes) per second.
    • Example @ 5 min: 1.7/sec
    • Example @ 10 min: 2.2/sec
  • Enemy HP: 50 × (1.15 ^ Minutes).
    • Scaling is compounding (+15% per minute).
    • Example @ 5 min: ~100 HP (2x)
    • Example @ 10 min: ~200 HP (4x)
    • Example @ 15 min: ~400 HP (8x)

Enemy Color Cycles (15-Minute Loop)

Enemies visual structure changes every 5 minutes in a repeating 15-minute loop.

Map Logic & Spawn Mechanics

2. Combat Ranges

  • Player Visibility: The camera sees a limited area, but enemies spawn slightly outside it.
  • Auto-Aim Area: 800px Radius. Player will automatically target the nearest enemy entering this zone.
  • Bullet Reach: ~1650px. You can manually aim and hit enemies immediately upon spawn.
