  1. PLAYER STATS & MECHANICS
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

BASE STATS (Level 1):
  Health (HP):           150
  Damage (DMG):          50
  Attack Speed (ATK):    500ms
  Regeneration (REG):    0.1 HP/frame
  Armor (ARM):           0
  
DERIVED STATS:
  Projectiles (MULTI):   1 (base)
  Pierce:                1 (base)
  Drones:                0 (base)
  
ARMOR FORMULA:
  Defense Reduction = 0.95 × (ARM / (ARM + 5263))
  Maximum Reduction = 95%
  
STAT CALCULATION:
  Final Value = (Base + Flat) × (1 + Mult/100)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  2. ENEMY DAMAGE SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

COLLISION DAMAGE (Body Contact):
  Formula: 30% of Enemy's Current Max HP
  Example: Enemy with 100 HP → 30 raw damage to player
  Scaling: Automatically scales with enemy HP progression
  
BOSS PROJECTILE DAMAGE:
  Spread Pattern (Type 0):  15 damage per projectile (5 projectiles)
  Tracking Snipe (Type 1):  25 damage per projectile (1 projectile)
  Attack Interval:          2500ms (2.5 seconds)
  
DAMAGE APPLICATION:
  All damage is reduced by player's armor stat
  Actual Damage = Raw Damage × (1 - Defense Reduction)

═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════
  5. THREE-LAYER SHELL SYSTEM
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

SHELL LAYERS:
  1. Core (Heart)       - Brightest, most saturated, represents life force
  2. Inner Shell        - Semi-transparent ring, slight glow
  3. Outer Shell        - Thin, sharp, bright edge, strong glow

SHELL ACTIVATION (Per Shape Repeat):
  1st Appearance:  Core dominant only
  2nd Appearance:  Core + Inner Shell active
  3rd+ Appearance: All three shells (Outer Shell dominant)

HP SCALING:
  Each shell layer adds durability/HP (exact multipliers TBD)

  8. ENEMY HP & SPEED SCALING (EXISTING SYSTEM)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

CURRENT IMPLEMENTATION:
  Cycle Count = floor(gameTime / 30)
  
  HP Multiplier:    1.15^cycleCount
  Speed Multiplier: 1 + (cycleCount × 0.02)
  Size Growth:      10 + (cycleCount × 1.5) pixels
  
  Base Standard HP: 50 × hpMult
  Boss HP:          15 × Base Standard HP

SPAWN RATE:
  Base Rate:        1.5 + (floor(gameTime / 60) × 0.1)
  Per Frame Chance: currentRate / 60
